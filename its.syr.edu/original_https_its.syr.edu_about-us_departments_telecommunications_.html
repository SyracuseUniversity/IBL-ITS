Telecommunications

What We Do Link

Telecommunications oversees and coordinates voice communication, cable television services, and accounts for the University community. The team’s responsibilities include landline and mobile/cellular maintenance, phone center operator services, voicemail systems, call center systems, emergency notification, campus directory coordination, and cable television.
